Policies

Please read our policies below.

  • Cancellations

    Full refunds are made on cancellations made one week or more prior to the event. Prorated refunds are made on cancellations made less than one week prior to the event.

  • Pick-up

    All items must be clean, neatly stacked and returned to their original containers. If pick up requires special circumstances,I.E same-day pick up, after hours pick-up,ect, there may be an additional charge.

  • Handling of Equipment

    Raj Party Rentals does not allow the use of staples or tape on any equipment. All items are expected to be returned in the same condition as it was rented. The charge for staple or tape removal from tables is $10.00 min. We appreciate your cooperation!

  • Rain Policy

    It is the customer's responsibility to cover and protect all rental equipment from bad weather. If items are damaged due to rain the customer will be liable for all damages.
